About Gotha Jaane

"Gotha Jaane" is a long-standing QG SIGNALS tradition that began during the Hong Kong era, serving as a meaningful get-together for QG SIGNALS officer, their families, and past members now serving in the Royal Signals. In Nepali, "Gotha" means a gathering or resting place, and "Jaaney" means to go, together symbolising "going to a place of togetherness." More than a social event, it includes the Gurkha Major's AGM and a general meeting focused on the betterment of the Paltan, strengtheninig regimental pride, unity, and continuity across generation.

Gotha Jaane 25

Gotha Jaane 25 is a highly anticipated annual event for all the serving QG SIGNALS Officers’ Club members and their families. This year the event will be held at MoD Stafford over the period of 22 – 24 Aug 25. The event will consist of golf competition, social evening, recreational activities, a formal Dinner Night and Annual General Meeting.
