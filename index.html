About Gotha Jane

Ex GJ 25 is a highly anticipated annual event for all the serving QG SIGNALS Officers’ Club members and their families. This year the event will be held at MoD Stafford over the period of 22 – 24 Aug 25. The event will consist of golf competition, social evening, recreational activities, a formal Dinner Night and Annual General Meeting.
